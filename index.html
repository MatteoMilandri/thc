About CTHC

We started meeting up in 2016 to limit the impact of a common problem that engineers face - talking shop at social engagements

.

Our initial goal is to be a forum for those interested in discussing electronics as it pertains to startups and small businesses, especially regarding the potential for these to scale rapidly.
